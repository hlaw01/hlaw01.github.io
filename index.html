About

I am currently serving as a Principal Research Engineer in the Network Technology Lab and am proud to bring my expertise to HUAWEI. Prior to joining the team, I earned my Ph.D. degree in Electrical & Computer Engineering from the National University of Singapore (NUS). My research interests lie in the fields of computer network systems, computer architecture, as well as network-application integration. The central focus of my research is to gain a comprehensive understanding of the design space tradeoffs in distributed systems. In particular, I have interest in the utilization of network equipment such as programmable switches or smart NICs, to boost the performance of upper-layer applications, including High-Performance Computing (HPC), distributed machine learning, and big data analysis. I am proud to have had some of my research implemented in computing and storage product lines, helping to revolutionize the way we approach these complex systems.
