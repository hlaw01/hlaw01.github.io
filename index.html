About

Xijun has received master degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, P.R. China, in 2018. Currently, he is a senior researcher of HUAWEI Noah’s Ark Lab and working towards his Ph.D. degree in the University of Science and Technology of China (HUAWEI-USTC Joint Ph.D. Program) under the supervision of Prof. Jie Wang. He has published several papers on top peer-reviewed conferences and journals (SIGMOD, KDD, ICDE, DAC, CIKM, ICDCS, TCYB, etc.). He has also been committee member of MDM 2021 Industry Track and reviewer of multiple top journals and conferences (TKDE, AAAI, ICDE, CIKM, etc.). Moreover, his research interests focus on learning to optimize combinatorial optimization problem and machine learning for computer systems.
