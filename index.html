About

Xijun is a senior researcher of HUAWEI Noah’s Ark Lab. Before that, he has received M.Sc. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, P.R. China, in 2018. He is working towards his Ph.D. degree in the University of Science and Technology of China (HUAWEI-USTC Joint Ph.D. Program) under the supervision of Prof. Jie Wang. He has published 10 papers on top peer-reviewed conferences and journals (such as SIGMOD, KDD, ICDE, DAC, CIKM, ICDCS, TCYB, etc.) and applied/published 12 patents (first author in 6 patents) with Noah's Ark Lab. And he has won the championship of student learderboard in Dual Track of NeurIPS’21 ML4CO Competition. His recent research interests focus on Mathematical Programming Solver, Learning to Optimization (L2O) and Machine Learning for Computer System (ML4CS). Besides, he is one of core developers of HUAWEI OptVerse AI Solver.
